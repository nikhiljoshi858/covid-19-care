COVID-19 Care

The novel Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) has been affecting people all over the globe. Millions of people have been detected positive, thousands of them have died. But even after 6 months from the date of detection of the first patient, no specific medication has been found to cure the disease.

Below we present a rough overview of the total patients, deaths and recovered cases in India

NOTE: The given data is until 1st July 2020
COVID-19 Patients Review

Fig. 1: A review of the total cases in India


As of July 1st 2020, the total cases in India reported were 5,85,792 and the numbers go on increasing day by day. The first patient was detected in mid February and in almost 3.5 months India already ranks 4th in terms of the most affected country.

COVID-19 Deaths Review

Fig. 2: A review of the deaths in India.


The total deaths in India are 17,410 as of 1st July 2020. The death rate in India is roughly 5% which is good considering the population India has.

COVID-19 Recovered Patients Review

Fig. 3 : A review of the recovered cases in India


The total count of recovered cases in India is 3,47,979 which is roughly 59.4% which is a good news.

From this data it is clear that the virus has been causing all sorts of problems in India in terms of economy, livelihood and so on. Even after 6 months from the date of the first patient detected in India, there is no specific vaccine or medication available against this disease. The only medication is to take precaution by following the Government norms of wearning face masks and maintaining social distancing in crowded public places. In this project we propose a method to detect if people are following social distancing and wearning face masks or not in images, videos and webcam feeds.
