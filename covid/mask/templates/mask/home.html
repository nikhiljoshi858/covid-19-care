Face masks are surgical masks designed to cover your mouth and nose from harmful substances like smoke, fog and aerial germs. In these tough times, it is of utmost importance to wear face masks wherever you go to be safe from the Coronavirus.

Face masks can protect people from the infection by not allowing the aerial germs to enter our mouth or nose. Since the virus affects the respiratory system, wearing a proper face mask is necessary to remain safe. The disease transmits either through coughing or sneezing or through touch. Face masks protects us from all these causes by not allowing any foreign particle from entering our nose. It is not necessary to use the costly masks, even our regular cloth masks are sufficient, even though the surgical masks provide more protection because of the material they are made from.
